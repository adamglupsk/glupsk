Beckmans Bookland:
Graduation Projects
May 22-25, 2025

Bookland is a touring pop-up shop for books, graphic design, illustration, publishing and more from BFA graduates of Visual Communication. Bookland’s first stop will take place as part of Beckmans College of Design’s Graduation Exhibition this spring. Welcome!

Location

Traveling to Bookland is easy. Beckmans College of Design is located at Agavägen 54, Lidingö in eastern Stockholm. Take the metro's red line to Ropsten and the tram Lidingöbanan to AGA. From Ropsten, Nacka Strand or Nybroplan, you can travel by Ferry SL80 (The Book Boat) to Lidingö/Dalénum. Use SL's travel planner here! Bookland will take place in Beckmans' library, during the main Graduation Exhibition!

Why 'Bookland'?

'Bookland' refers to the country code represented in the ISBN (International Standard Book Number) used to register books and publications. On any published book, regardless of country of origin, the first three digits of '9 78' stand for Bookland. For the curious: Read more. We are turning this Bookland into a physical space where visitors are invited to read, explore or purchase works from Beckmans graduates. Books were the starting point for Bookland but visitors should expect much more than books in the traditional sense!
