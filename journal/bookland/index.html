Bookland is a touring pop-up shop for books, graphic design, illustrations, publishing and more from BFA graduates of Visual Communication. Bookland’s first stop will take place as part of Beckmans College of Design’s Graduation Exhibition this spring. Welcome!

Traveling to Bookland's premiere event is simple. Beckmans College of Design is located at Agavägen 54, Lidingö in eastern Stockholm. Take the line to Ropsten and the tram Lidingöbanan to AGA. From Ropsten and Nybroplan, you can travel by the Book Boat a.k.a. SL80 Ferry to Lidingö/Dalénum. Use SL's travel planner here!

Why 'Bookland'?

'Bookland' refers to the country code represented in the ISBN barcode-system used to register books and publications. We are turning this Bookland into a physical space where visitors are allowed to read, explore or purchase works from Beckmans graduates. While books is the starting point for Bookland, visitors can expect much more than books!
