There is no such thing as magic (2023)

In collaboration with Maja Ringsäter and Solvej Jansson, I designed an identity and theme for an exhibition showing artworks made by students from Beckmans College of Design, based on scientific research on emerging AI technology, conducted at The Beijer Institute (Stockholm). The title 'There is no such thing as magic' was based on insights from seminars with scientists. Society tends to describe AI as if it were something magical that came out of nowhere, something beyond our control. In reality, the processes behind what we call AI can be understood, questioned, and improved. To let the analogy stop at it being magic, is a mistake.

'The Cloud' is not a Cloud, it's cables.
