There is no such thing as magic

Visual identity + animation
There is no such thing as magic is a collaborative project about AI and its societal and environmental impact. The visual identity was designed to reflect the work of scientific researchers at the Beijer Institute and encourage reflection on the realities of generative AI. The project was exhibited at Svenskt Tenn in Stockholm, 2024.
Designed in collaboration with:
Maja Ringsäter + Solvej Jansson

Stars

The pattern graphics used in the identity was based on contemporary design trends in apps and websites. We noticed stars, often symbolic for magic, was used for features enabled by new AI technology.
